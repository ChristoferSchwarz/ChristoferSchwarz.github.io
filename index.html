Who am I?

Who am I?

You might wonder "who is this beautiful long haired man?". Great question!
My name is Christofer Schwarz, I’m a Digital Designer, Filmmaker and Web designer. When I was a prepubescent, shorter haired boy I discovered the wonders of image and video manipulation, and ever since I photoshopped my first image I was hooked.

If I’m not staring at a screen I’m probably with my girlfriend and her two pet mice, Mus-Mus and Kevin, hanging out with my friends or watching a Formula 1 Grand Prix.

They say "curiosity killed the cat", good thing I’m a human because I’m as curious as they come. If someone answers a question with "I don’t know", I’ll be the first person googling away on my phone.
But I digress.
